About Commerce Analytics

Commerce Analytics is a modern, multi-tenant retail intelligence platform built for data-driven businesses. It combines an automated ETL pipeline, AI-powered forecasting, and an interactive BI dashboard.

The 3-Fold System

🌐 Main Site

Public landing, registration, and onboarding. Users sign up, and admins review & approve accounts.

👤 Client Portal

Approved clients upload their sales data and access personalised analytics, forecasts, and reports.

🛡️ Admin Portal

Platform administrators manage users, monitor all clients, and view platform-wide analytics.

Key Features

📦 ETL Pipeline

Upload CSV or Excel files. The pipeline automatically extracts, cleans, and transforms your sales data into a normalised warehouse schema ready for analysis.

🗄️ Warehouse Schema

Each client gets an isolated SQLite database with tables for sales transactions, products, customers, regions, and categories — ensuring full data privacy.

🔮 Prophet Forecasting

Meta's Prophet model generates multi-horizon sales forecasts (14–90 days). Accuracy metrics include MAE, RMSE, and MAPE for model evaluation.

📊 BI Dashboard

Interactive Chart.js charts for sales trends, top products, category distribution, and regional breakdowns. KPI cards show revenue, orders, and customer insights at a glance.
